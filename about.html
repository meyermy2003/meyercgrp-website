Who We Are

Be human. That's our philosophy.

Practical ERP improvement. Human-centered consulting. That means building real partnerships, focusing on adoption, and delivering solutions that work in the real world — not just on paper.

We partner with mid-market and enterprise organizations to stabilize, optimize, and evolve their systems and processes — without forcing oversized consulting programs or one-time "big bang" transformations. Our senior-led team works alongside client stakeholders to deliver practical improvements that are adopted, sustainable, and aligned to both immediate needs and long-term strategy.

Founded by Kevin Meyer, our team is handpicked for expertise and commitment. With deep roots in PeopleSoft and the broader Oracle ecosystem, Kevin routinely travels to meet clients personally — because real partnerships aren't built remotely.
